THE ART OF JAPANESE CUISINE

Welcome to Nagi, where tradition meets modern elegance in the heart of New York City. Inspired by the refined artistry of Japanese cuisine, Nagi blends the timeless techniques of washoku with a contemporary urban edge, creating an experience that is both sophisticated and authentic. Rooted in tradition yet forward-thinking in execution, our menu is a celebration of seasonality, craftsmanship, and purity of flavor. From exquisitely prepared omakase selections to expertly crafted nigiri and sashimi, each dish reflects the balance and precision that define Japanese culinary excellence.

The atmosphere at Nagi is designed to transport you. Our interior fuses minimalist aesthetics with warm, natural elements, featuring dark wood, soft ambient lighting, and subtle nods to Japanese architecture. The intimate yet vibrant setting makes it a destination for both discerning food lovers and those seeking a moment of quiet indulgence amid the city's energy. Located in the heart of New York, Nagi is more than a restaurant—it is an invitation to immerse yourself in the essence of Japanese culture, where every meal is an art form and every visit a journey. Whether you're here for an elegant dinner, a curated sake experience, or a moment of respite from the city's rush, we welcome you to discover the harmony of past and present, tradition and innovation—only at Nagi.
